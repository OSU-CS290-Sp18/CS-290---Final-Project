Tree Stump

Admin

Latitute: 44.609958

Longitute: -123.335086

A hollowed out old tree stump near the trailhead for oak creek. Look for the pink arrow on a rock near the fork in the trail. Be careful for poison oak when looking for the tree stump, and don't forget to respect nature and act respectfully.
